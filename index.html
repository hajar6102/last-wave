Welcome to Waves!

A student society passionate about programming, designing, and building robots, as well as developing artificial intelligence applications.

MISSION

VISION

OBJECTIVES

Empowering Tomorrow's Workforce

To engage and enhance the skills of a diverse pool of passionate and proficient students, aligning their competencies with the demands of the labor market, and collaborating with them on projects.

Igniting Passion

To foster a vibrant student community with a keen interest in artificial intelligence, particularly addressing the existing gap in this domain.

Driving Innovation

To facilitate the organization of competitions at both university and national levels, positioning the University of Jordan as a prominent hub for robotics and artificial intelligence competitions and technical endeavors within Jordan.

Bridging Theory and Practice

To invite industry experts and offer comprehensive courses covering the technical facets of robotics, ranging from design principles to coding proficiency.

TEAM

TECHNICAL TEAM

The Technical team is our innovative core, where all project building takes place. Our members specialize in programming, robotics, and technical workshops. Their commitment to excellence shapes our society.

LOGISTICS TEAM

The Logistics team serves as the backbone of Waves' events. Members ensure that operations run smoothly, managing fundraising, sponsors, and business connections, all vital for our events’ success.

SOCIAL MEDIA TEAM

The media team is the creative heartbeat of our society, capturing our events through their lens and sharing our achievements on social media. Members are responsible for content across all aspects of the society.

DEVELOPMENT TEAM

The Development Team is responsible for creating the Waves Society website. Members actively participate in both front and backend development, in addition to constant maintenance and enhancement.

ACTIVITIES

Daad & Smart.Eco

We are happy to announce our partnership with @daad_worldwide! As they provided us with support and funding that has helped us build a fire abatement drone, a robot arm, and other robots we are currently working on. We presented WAVES and talked more about our team and its projects at the Smart.Eco conference. We are excited to have more meetings with them in the future! 🌊🤖 We would like to thank our distinguished doctors for their help and support, and we especially thank Dr. Osama Harfoushi, Dr. Yazan Al-Zein, and Dr. Ruba JUST.

Jobedu

WAVES announces its partnership with Jobedu to provide WAVES team with custom-made shirts, along with a range of mutual benefits and interests for the upcoming period discussed during the meeting 🤫📑. We are thrilled about this collaboration and look forward to future opportunities🔥🌊.

Sager

We had a productive and insightful meeting with @sagerdrone today. 🚀✨ It was a great opportunity to get to know their team better and discuss the intersection of AI and robotics, particularly how these technologies are advancing the capabilities of drones. We greatly appreciated their expert advice on our upcoming projects, which provided us with valuable perspectives. We look forward to implementing their suggestions and advancing our initiatives! 🌊

HTU - Sumo contest

Waves is excited to announce its members’ participation in the 2024 Sumo Robot Competition at @htujo . Team Waves01 reached the top 32 teams out of 220 by beating a number of strong opponents. It was an amazing experience filled with learning, fun, and intense robot battles. Special thanks to Professors Ruba Obiedat and Ali Rodan for their invaluable guidance and support throughout this journey. Can’t wait for next year’s competition!
